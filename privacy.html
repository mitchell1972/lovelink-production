LoveLink ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address and name when you create an account
  • Profile Information: Profile photo (optional)
  • User Content: Photos you share in Moments, mood check-ins, gratitude entries, and messages exchanged with your partner
  • Usage Data: App interactions such as pulse signals and session completions
  • Partner Connection: Information about your connection with your partner within the app

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the LoveLink service
  • Connect you with your partner through unique link codes
  • Enable sharing of photos, moods, and messages between partners
  • Send notifications about partner activity (with your permission)
  • Improve and optimize our app

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Your data is shared only:

  • With your partner: Content you create (photos, moods, messages) is shared with your connected partner
  • Service providers: We use Supabase for secure data storage and authentication
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which provides:

  • Encrypted data transmission (TLS/SSL)
  • Secure cloud storage
  • Row-level security ensuring you only access your own data

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Disconnect: Unlink from your partner at any time
  • Export: Request your data in a portable format

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account:

  • Your profile and personal data will be deleted within 30 days
  • Shared content (moments, messages) may remain visible to your former partner

7. Children's Privacy

LoveLink is not intended for users under 17 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 17.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
